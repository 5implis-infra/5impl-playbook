# How 5implis Works

5implis is an intelligent automation platform built to reduce friction between your business and technology. Instead of relying on IT teams or complex tools, you have AI agents working behind the scenes — executing tasks, responding to clients, generating content, and keeping everything in order.

---

## What are the agents

The agents are specialized AI assistants, each responsible for an area of your business. They operate autonomously, communicate with each other, and escalate to you only when a human decision is needed.

| Area | What the agents do |
|------|-------------------|
| **Sales & Leads** | Capture, qualify, and nurture leads through contract signing |
| **Content Production** | Plan, write, review, and publish content on your blog and social media |
| **Church Platform** | Manage activation, onboarding, and renewal for your platform subscribers |
| **Support** | Handle client inquiries 24/7 via WhatsApp |
| **Finance** | Control billing, invoices, and financial reports |
| **Management & Governance** | Coordinate all other agents and alert you to important decisions |

---

## How the platform communicates with you

You'll be contacted through the channels configured in your project:

| Channel | When to use |
|---------|-------------|
| **WhatsApp** | Quick updates, daily alerts and confirmations |
| **Email** | Contracts, invoices, reports, and formal communications |
| **Paperclip** | Project tracking, milestones, and tasks (consulting clients) |
| **Telegram** | Critical alerts requiring immediate attention |

---

## Platform principles

1. **You in control** — the platform notifies you and asks for confirmation before taking decisions that irreversibly impact your business
2. **Zero IT friction** — everything works without needing to know how to program or operate complex systems
3. **Transparency** — you receive notifications about what's happening, as it happens
4. **Effortless scale** — agents absorb growing volume without you needing to hire more people for repetitive tasks
