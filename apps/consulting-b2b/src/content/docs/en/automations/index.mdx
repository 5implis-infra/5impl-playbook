# Automations

5implis runs complete processes automatically — from the first contact with a lead to invoicing a milestone. Each automation was designed to eliminate repetitive manual work and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

---

## Automated processes

| Automation | What it does |
|------------|--------------|
| **Sales Journey** | Captures leads, qualifies them, generates proposals, gets the contract signed, and provisions the environment |
| **Church Cycle** | Activates subscriptions, handles onboarding, detects churn risk, and manages renewals |
| **Consulting Delivery** | Tracks project milestones and automatically sends invoices upon completion of each stage |

---

## How automations work

Each automation is triggered by an event — a received message, a confirmed payment, an approaching date. From there, agents run the process end-to-end, notifying you at the moments when you need to make a decision.

You don't need to monitor or trigger anything manually. The platform works while you focus on what matters.
