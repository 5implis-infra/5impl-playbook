# Onboarding — Church Platform

After your subscription is activated, you receive a sequence of messages and contacts over 10 days. Here's what to expect at each stage.

---

## Your journey in the first 10 days

| Day | Channel | What arrives |
|-----|---------|-------------|
| **Day 0** | WhatsApp | Welcome message with access link and temporary credentials |
| **Day 2** | WhatsApp | Tip for registering your first members |
| **Day 5** | Email | Financial module setup guide |
| **Day 7** | WhatsApp | Check-in — we're here if you have questions |
| **Day 10** | Email | Invitation for a 20-minute success call |

---

## What you'll receive at each touchpoint

### Day 0 — WhatsApp (immediately after activation)

```
Hi, {your name}! 👋

Welcome to the Church Platform! Your platform is now active.

🔗 Access here: {platform link}
👤 Email: {your email}
🔑 Temporary password: {password}

Where to start:
1. Log in and change your password
2. Set up your church information
3. Register the first members

Questions? We're here! Reply to this message any time.
```

### Day 2 — WhatsApp

```
Hi, {your name}! How's it going?

A quick tip: the most important thing to start is registering your members.

📋 Quick tutorial → {tutorial link}

Any questions, just ask! 🙌
```

### Day 5 — Email

**Subject:** Setting up the finances for \{your church name\}

```
Hi, {your name}!

Now that you're familiar with the platform, it's time to set up the financial module.

What you can do:
✅ Set up your chart of accounts
✅ Register donation categories
✅ Configure financial campaigns

Complete guide: {guide link}

Any questions, reply to this email.
```

### Day 7 — WhatsApp

```
Hi, {your name}! It's been a week since you activated the Church Platform.

How has the experience been? Is there anything I can help you with?

Reply here and we'll help you in minutes! 💬
```

### Day 10 — Email

**Subject:** Want to hop on a quick call? ☕

```
Hi, {your name}!

You've completed your first week on the Church Platform! 🎉

We want to understand how your experience has been and make sure you're getting
the most out of everything the platform offers.

How about a 20-minute call?

📅 Schedule here: {Calendly link}

If you prefer, you can reply to this email with your questions.
```

---

## How to customize these messages

All the texts above are editable. If you want to adjust the tone, the links, or include information specific to your community, contact us through [support](/en/support/) and we'll make the changes at no additional cost.
