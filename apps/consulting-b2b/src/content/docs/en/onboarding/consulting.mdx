# Onboarding — Consulting

After the contract is signed, 5implis gets to work immediately. Here's what happens at each phase.

---

## Phase 1 — Right after signing

As soon as the contract is digitally signed, you receive a confirmation email and the team starts preparing your environment.

### Signing confirmation email

**Subject:** Contract signed — welcome to 5implis! 🎉

```
Hi, {your name}!

Your contract has been successfully signed. We're thrilled to have you as a 5implis client!

What happens now:
1. Our team will prepare your work environment
2. Within 24 business hours you'll receive your access credentials
3. We'll schedule our kickoff call

Your project summary:
{list of contracted services}

Monthly value: $ {amount}

Questions? Reply to this email or reach us on WhatsApp.
```

---

## Phase 2 — Environment ready (within 24 business hours)

After preparation, you receive access credentials for your systems and a kickoff call invitation.

### Welcome email

**Subject:** Your environment is ready — let's get started!

```
Hi, {your name}!

Your automation environment is configured and ready to use.

🔑 You'll receive the following access credentials:
- Project management system
- Workflow automation platform
- Data and integrations dashboard

📋 Next steps:
1. Access the systems and explore
2. Reply to this email to schedule our kickoff call
3. Prepare access to the tools we'll be integrating

We're excited to get started! 🚀
```

---

## Kickoff — the first meeting

In the kickoff call, we'll:

- Review the contracted scope point by point
- Understand your current systems (CRMs, spreadsheets, emails, etc.)
- Define project milestones and timelines
- Agree on the communication protocol during the project

---

## How we track the project

| Channel | For what |
|---------|----------|
| **Management system (Paperclip)** | Development issues, milestones, technical decisions |
| **WhatsApp** | Informal communications, quick demos, reminders |
| **Email** | Invoices, contracts, formal communications |
| **Telegram** | Alerts when a milestone is completed |

---

## Satisfaction survey (30 days)

One month after project start, you receive a quick NPS survey to tell us how the experience is going. It's short, takes less than 2 minutes, and your feedback directly guides the improvements we make.
