# Integrations

The 5implis platform connects to the tools you already use and those that are part of your operation. Below is an overview of each integration and what it's used for.

---

## Connected tools

| Tool | What it does for your business |
|------|-------------------------------|
| **WhatsApp (Zernio)** | Communication with leads and clients — automated messages and support |
| **Payment Gateway** | Recurring billing, payment confirmation, delinquency management |
| **Digital Signature** | Sending and collecting signatures on contracts and proposals |
| **Cloudflare** | Automatic website publishing after new content is created |
| **Canva** | Image generation for posts and newsletter from your brand templates |
| **Google Search Console** | Search ranking monitoring to guide content production |
| **Telegram** | Internal alerts for you about critical operational events |
| **Coolify** | Infrastructure for Church Platform environments (automatic provisioning) |
| **LiteLLM** | Management of AI usage quotas per client |

---

## How integrations work

Each tool is connected once, during your project setup, and then operates automatically. You don't need to log into any of these tools for the platform to work — the agents take care of everything.

If an integration needs adjustment or credential updates, the 5implis team handles the process.
